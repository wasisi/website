Our Mission: An online platform to coffee farmers, buyers and researchers to network and access market information

We care about coffee and the wellbeing of the hardworking farmers and people who consume, trade, promote and research about it. We believe that access to market information will help farmers participate in decision making and profit sharing.

How we do it

We use artificial intelligence to collect, clean and store transaction reports from the Nairobi Coffee Exchange.

We analyse the transaction reports and provide and interface to query our database for customised charts and information.

We build and maintain a directory of coffee dealers, producers, marketers and regulators to help in traceability and networking.

We maintain a glossary of terms to help users navigate the coffee world in Kenya.
