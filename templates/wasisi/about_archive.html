How We Do It

We provide the infrastructure so that third party educators and educational institutes can develop their courses.

We develop our own learning materials as a mixture of free and open online resources selected by expert educators and material developed by us.

We focus on designing a self-paced learning experience that comes as close as possible to what you would learn in the college classroom. Our courses are available to you any time, and the deadlines are under your control.
